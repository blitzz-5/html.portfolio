The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all time

Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back

This is the best of the Star Wars films. It is brilliantly directed and has amazing story telling elements. It is quite possibly the best Star Wars film ever.

Indiana Jones and The Raiders of The Lost Ark

This is the first of the Indiana Jones films. Some of the cinemetography is industry changing and the story telling is fantastic!

The Lord of the Rings (All of them)

This entire film series brings the storys to life from the book. Peter Jackson brilliantly directed it and change the way movies were made with the technology they pioneered
